Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb grated sharp cheddar cheese
  • 4 tbs softened butter
  • 1 c sifted all purpose flour
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°
  2. Mix all ingredients and roll into marble sized balls
  3. Bake for 10-12 minutes on a cookie sheet
